About GeoTech Solutions

Excellence in GIS equipment and services

Our Story

Founded in Nairobi by Javan Meshack, a geomatics engineering and geospatial data science graduate, GeoTech Solutions was established to address the growing demand for high-quality GIS equipment and expert services in East Africa.

With over a decade of industry experience, our team understands the unique challenges facing geospatial professionals in the region and is committed to providing solutions that empower our clients to achieve exceptional results.

Our Mission

To provide cutting-edge geospatial technologies and expert services that enable our clients to make informed decisions, optimize operations, and drive sustainable development across East Africa.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of GIS solutions in East Africa, recognized for our technical excellence, innovation, and commitment to advancing geospatial capabilities in the region.
